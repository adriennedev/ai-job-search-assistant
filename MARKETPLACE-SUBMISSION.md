# Claude Plugin Directory — Submission Fields

- **Link to plugin:** https://github.com/adriennedev/ai-job-search-assistant
- **Plugin homepage:** https://github.com/adriennedev/ai-job-search-assistant
- **Plugin name:** AI Job Search Assistant
- **Plugin description:** An AI-augmented job search pipeline that reviews job postings weekly, scores them against a fit rubric you define, keeps a tracker spreadsheet current, and generates tailored resumes and cover letters. It learns from your observed interview rates to sharpen targeting over time. It never applies to jobs on your behalf — every deliverable stops at "ready for your review."
- **Example use cases:**
  - Example 1: "Run the weekly job search" — Claude reviews saved and new postings, scores each against your rubric, updates your tracker, drafts tailored materials for the top 3 matches, and emails you a summary with concrete suggestions.
  - Example 2: "Tailor my resume for this posting" — Claude reads the job posting, mirrors its language in your resume, writes a 4-paragraph cover letter in your voice with one honestly acknowledged gap, and saves both as .docx.
  - Example 3: "I got an interview at Acme, update the tracker" — Claude logs the status change, recalculates your funnel (applied / no-response / interview / offer), and surfaces one pattern worth acting on.
